Whataburger is known for its delicious and freshly made burgers, and their lunch menu offers a variety of mouth-watering options. Whether you’re craving a classic burger, a hearty chicken sandwich, or a flavorful meal combo, Whataburger has something for everyone.

🍔 Popular Whataburger Lunch Items

  • Whataburger – The signature burger made with 100% fresh beef, lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and mustard.
  • Double Meat Whataburger – A double-patty burger for those who love extra meat.
  • Whataburger Patty Melt – A delicious burger with grilled onions and creamy pepper sauce on Texas Toast.
  • Grilled Chicken Sandwich – A healthy and tasty option with a grilled chicken breast, fresh veggies, and wheat bun.
  • Honey BBQ Chicken Strip Sandwich – Crispy chicken strips tossed in sweet and smoky BBQ sauce.

🍟 Combo Meals

All lunch combos include a medium-sized drink and a side of Whataburger’s famous fries.
